The Now Playing app shows the current playing song on Apple Music or Spotify, updating every 5 seconds, being a simple and minimalist widget on the status bar.

## FAQ

### Q. Do I need to link my Spotify or Apple Music account?

A. No, the status bar widget uses local data to determine the current song playing.

### Q. Can I use the Spotify or Apple Music browser apps?

A. No, as the widget uses local app data

### Q. What platforms are supported?

A. MacOS Tahoe 26 Or Later
